📜 Nomination Guide

How it works
1

Members with the roles Ascendant, bitty, ritty, Ritualist, or Radiant Ritualist are allowed to nominate members for roles. Those with the Initiate role cannot nominate.

2

Each eligible member can nominate only one person for one role.

3

The community vote is live, and everyone in the server can participate by voting. Each person may vote once for each role.
